Privacy Policy

Simple. Transparent. Secure.

This bot collects and stores NO data.

Data Processing

emojimage operates entirely in memory to process your request. Once the link is extracted and sent back to the Discord channel, the data is instantly cleared and thrown away.

Message Deletion

You have full control over your chat history. In a 1-on-1 DM with the bot, use the /cleardms command to securely bulk-delete its recent messages sent to you. In group chats or friend DMs, Discord natively protects your privacy by blocking bots from reading group chat history; to delete a bot's message there, you can simply right-click (or hold) the bot's message and click "Delete Message" yourself.

No Logging

We do not maintain logs of what emojis you extract, which servers you use the bot in, or who you are. Because the bot is self-hostable, you have 100% visibility into the source code to verify this.
